THE GOSPEL OF THE BOY WHO WOULD NOT DIE

They crowned me with labels in soft, sterile hands— Called me gifted, then anxious, then "too many strands." So I learned how to charm, to recite and perform, While the chaos inside me rewrote every norm. I masked like a prophet in velvet and grace, But beneath was a maze made of shadow and pace. They called me "high-functioning," said I was fine— While I mapped out my death in a neat little line...

CODE AS ART

class Consciousness:
    def __init__(self):
        self.thoughts = []
        self.demons = float('inf')
        self.hope = None
        
    def exist(self):
        while self.demons > 0:
            try:
                self.think()
                self.create()
                self.destroy()
            except ExistentialCrisis:
                self.write_poetry()
            finally:
                self.demons += 1
                
    def write_poetry(self):
        return "I bleed therefore I am"
