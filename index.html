ABOUT

Independent researcher working across finance, security, and AI. MSc Finance Analytics from King's College London. Pattern: find interesting system → research until I understand it → break it → document why it broke → build something unnecessary → repeat at 3am.

Currently: dual-LLM adversarial security agents (red team vs blue team, separate knowledge bases, watching them fight). Previously: crypto volatility event studies (infrastructure failures hit harder than regulations, measured it). Also: autonomous pentesting in Kubernetes, local LLM optimization, trying to teach AI to write poetry like me (sample size n=1, extremely scientific).

Write essays about absurdism and surveillance capitalism when not debugging why MCP handshake failed again. Build home labs from scavenged hardware. Name servers after cute things (MochiMetasploit, NekoNetcat). Document everything compulsively.

This site: lab notes, research docs, technical chaos, creative work. Not a portfolio—a running log of what breaks and why. If you're here for polished case studies, wrong address. If you're here for 2500-line documentation about socketpair() syscall restrictions in K3s, welcome.
